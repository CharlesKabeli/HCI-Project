Sesotho Teaching & Learning Resources Online

The aim of this page is to provide, in addition to the other pages of this site, classroom resources in Sesotho (Southern Sotho). This language is studied as Home Language, First and Second Additional Language at schools throughout South Africa.

Here are some basic work sheets, summaries and resources that can be used in the classroom. Feel free to send in your own resources to the e-mail address at the bottom of this page.

Also see the online exercises available for Sesotho learners.
